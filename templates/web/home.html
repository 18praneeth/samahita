    Swadhaar is a Social Support Networking initiative to enable Konkani Talents, Budding Entrepreneurs, Students, Gifted Children, Senior Citizens, Konkani Institutions etc. to realise their fullest potential by providing Consultation, Training and a result oriented network platform.

    TWO TIER NETWORKING

    Swadhaar is a network of grass root level Social Support Units â€“ a group of socially responsive, committed young Konkani people. The Social Support units will constantly keep in touch with the people of their chosen area and escalate the Cases to the central level. They will also implement central level programmes such as Gifted Child hunt, Talent Outreach, Entrepreneur Outreach etc.

    The Central Level will have a control room which will facilitate the resolution of cases escalated by the Social Support Units. The Control room will have empaneled mentors, consultants and need assessors. When a case is escalated to the central level, the control room will assign a need assessor and assess the need pertaining to the case. On the basis of Need Assessment report the Control Room will assign consultants, mentors and enablers to the cases. Thus a case from the grassroot level get addressed quickly and resolved in timely manner.

    SOCIAL SUPPORT SEGMENTS

    The following are a few broad segments to which the Swadhaar will provide enabling services.

    • Gifted Child Programme
      Annually a Gifted Child Hunt will be organized by the central level and implemented through SSUs by way of Gifted Child Tests. Identified Gifted Children will be provided with 360 degree support ensuring the realization of fullest potential of the child.
    • Talent Management Support
      The Talents identified by the SSUs such as talented young persons in Music, Dance, Arts, Literature, Sports and Games, Coding etc will be groomed by providing mentoring, social media assistance and travel grants.
    • Budding Entrepreneurs Support
      The struggling entrepreneurs identified by the SSUs will be provided with Consultation in compliances, know-how, finance and Branding.
    • Startup Support
      The young people with Start Up ideas screened by the SSUs for passion and originality, will be vetted and facilitated with training, compliance and seed funding to start up their venture.
    • Student Support
      Academically bright students identified by the SSUs will be supported through Scholarships and Skill Development Programme.
    • Senior Citizen Support
      The unassisted Senior Citizens identified by the SSUs will be provided with 360 Degree support to ensure their wellbeing and dignified life.
    • Chronic Illness Support
      The Chronic illness cases identified by the SSUs will be supported with Review of Medical Diagnosis, ameliorate the medical care standards, Care Giver Trainings and all possible aid.
    • Government Scheme Support
      The eligible candidates identified by the SSUs and will be assisted for availing Government Scheme benefits.
    • De-Addiction and rehabilitation Support
      The identified cases will be assisted with support to rehabilitate.
    • Mental Wellness Programmes
      The SSUs will undertake a mental wellness survey to findout the possible reasons and evolve a mechanism for the social corrections.
    • Women Empowerment
      The VSSN will organize workshops for women empowerment in areas such as skill development, post-maternity career planning etc.
    • Support to Konkani Institutions
      Institutions of Konkani Communities will be provided with consultation to comply with Government Norms, Technology Implementation etc.
